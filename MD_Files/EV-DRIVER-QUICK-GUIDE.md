# EV Driver Quick Guide

## How to Charge Your Vehicle

### Step 1: Authorize
**Tap your RFID card** on the charger's card reader
- Green light = Authorized
- Red light = Not authorized (contact support)

### Step 2: Connect
**Plug the charging cable** into your vehicle
- Charger will start automatically
- Blue light = Charging in progress

### Step 3: Charge
**Monitor progress** on charger display
- Shows power (kW) and energy (kWh)
- Leave vehicle until charging complete

### Step 4: Disconnect
**When finished:**
1. Tap your RFID card again to stop
2. Unplug cable from vehicle
3. Return cable to holster
4. Green light = Ready for next user

## FAQs

**How long does charging take?**
Depends on your vehicle and current battery level. Typical session: 1-4 hours

**How much will it cost?**
Cost = (Energy in kWh × Rate) + Session Fee
Example: 30 kWh × $0.30 + $1.00 = $10.00

**Can I leave my vehicle overnight?**
Check location policy - some allow, others don't

**What if I forget to stop?**
Charging stops automatically when battery full
